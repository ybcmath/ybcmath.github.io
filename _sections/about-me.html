I just received my PhD in applied math at UCLA. I graduated with a B.S. in Math from Zhejiang University.

I worked on large-scale recommendation systems and spatio-temporal data modeling from human activities such as social network via deep multivariate self-exicting point process. This work was supported by a National Institute of Justice Graduate Research Fellowship (GRF-STEM). I also working on time series analysis in medical imaging and seismology using machine learning. Some of this work has been in conjunction with Los Alamos National Laboratory. My PhD advisor is Andrea Bertozzi
