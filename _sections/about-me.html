I'm a fourth-year graduate student at UCLA, working in applied math with Andrea Bertozzi.

I'm currently modeling spatio-temporal data from human activities such as violent crimes and social network via multivariate self-exicting point process. I also working on time series analysis in medical imaging and seismology using machine learning. Some of this work has been in conjunction with Los Alamos National Laboratory.
