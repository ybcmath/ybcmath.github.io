I'm a final-year graduate student at UCLA, working in applied math with Andrea Bertozzi. I graduated with a B.S. in Math from Zhejiang University.

I'm currently working on large-scale spatio-temporal data modeling from human activities such as violent crimes and social network via deep multivariate self-exicting point process. This work was supported by a National Institute of Justice Graduate Research Fellowship (GRF-STEM). I also working on time series analysis in medical imaging and seismology using machine learning. Some of this work has been in conjunction with Los Alamos National Laboratory.
